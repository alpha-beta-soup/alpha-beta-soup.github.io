MetOcean is where I got my first full time job after graduating university. I was initially brought on to develop plugins for desktop GIS tools, but worked my way onto the web development team.

I had to learn fast, and this is where I first used React and AWS, and learnt about test-driven development.

I also developed back-end services for geospatial data, such as tropical cyclones, lightning strikes (using a WebSocket server), and weather map tiles. These were then visualised in our interactive maps applications for use by customers.

I loved living in Raglan and travel back often for summer holidays.
