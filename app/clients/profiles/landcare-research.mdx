I am currently employed by Landcare Research, a Crown Research Institute, as a Geospatial Analyst. I bring my experience working with geospatial technology to Landcare Research's domain expertise in soil science, land-use modelling, and ecology.

Here, I have the opportunity to use the High Performance Computing Facility available at another CRI: NIWA. I like to write reproducible scientific workflows using the open source workflow management system, [Snakemake](https://snakemake.readthedocs.io/en/stable/). This has the benefit of allowing for near-identical execution of code between a desktop environment and in the cluster.

I have written and contributed to several academic papers during my time here. Check out [my ORCID profile](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-7400-2530) for the latest.
