During my time at university, I worked part-time for the NZ ISCR as a research assistant. I was particularly heavily involved in a project which investigated commute mode decisions (whether to drive, or walk, etc.) and home ownership decisions contingent on this commute, but also on householders' access to schools of different qualities. I helped to assemble the relevant spatial information for this project.

I was involved in other, non-spatial projects, too. I particularly remember one where I reviewed life-cycle savings behaviour, which turned me into a saver.
